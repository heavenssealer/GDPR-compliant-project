1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They allow us to recognize your browser and store certain information.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Necessary cookies: These are essential for the website to function (e.g. security, network management, accessibility).
  • Analytics cookies: These help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it. Data is anonymized where possible.
  • Marketing cookies: These are used to deliver advertisements relevant to you and your interests (only with your consent).
  • Partners cookies: These allow data sharing with trusted partners for joint services or marketing purposes.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Provide and secure our services
  • Remember your preferences
  • Measure and improve website performance
  • Deliver personalized content and ads (if you consent)
